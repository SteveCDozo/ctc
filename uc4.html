Task Recruitment through Crowdsourced Employees

TARGETED ISSUE

Finding employees to work on new tasks can be tough

Small and medium sized businesses without an experienced workforce may find themselves requesting help or outsourcing work to bigger companies with more knowledge in the field. Managers of big companies often have trouble finding an employee to work on these new client requests or tasks. With over 1,000 employees at a big company and numerous requests to be taken care of, it may take quite some time to send out emails and wait for responses.
SOLUTION PURPOSE

Find unused talent in your corporation

Small and medium sized businesses need to get sustainable and constant help for various cybersecurity issues. Specific issues they may need assistance with include: reacting to and preventing various malware, encrypting data, and writing strong security policies.
Big consulting companies need to have convenient and efficient ways to find employees for their clients. They need to be able to handle an increase in client requests so they can generate more profit.
Our solution aims to facilitate the process of assigning employees to new client requests by introducing an internal recruitment system.
SOLUTION PROCESS

How will the system be used?

  • 1
    Client Makes a Request

    A client submits a new request for a certain task.

  • 2
    Request Gets Posted

    A manager can post the client's new request to the board provided by the system.

  • 3
    Preferences Are Set

    The manager can set any preferences for the request such as location, language, skills, and time availability.

  • 4
    Employees Apply

    Employees of the company will be able to view and apply for the task on the board. The manager can then choose one of the applicants.

HIGHLIGHTS

Benefits you'll value

Verified Crowd

All users of the system need to be verified as employees of the company.

Easy to Use

Employees can find jobs faster and managers can filter and sort through applicants.

Profit

Profits will either come through commission or subscription fees for using the service.
